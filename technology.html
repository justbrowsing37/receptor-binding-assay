Receptor binding assays have evolved with technology. Modern methods include:

  • Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR): Measures biomolecular interactions in real-time.
  • Fluorescence Polarization (FP): Assesses molecular binding through light polarization.
  • Bio-layer Interferometry (BLI): Optical technique for kinetic analysis.
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): Automates data analysis and predictive modeling.
  • High-Throughput Screening (HTS): Rapidly tests thousands of compounds.
