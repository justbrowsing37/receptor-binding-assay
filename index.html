Welcome

On this site, I explore the world of receptor binding assays through the interconnected lenses of science, technology, society, and the environment. My goal is to present a clear and balanced understanding of how these assays function at a molecular level, the innovations shaping their evolution, and their broader impact on public health and the planet. I aim to highlight both the opportunities and challenges they present, offering insights into their practical applications and long-term implications.
